Welcome to Sea Flare, a realm where dining transcends expectations. Our vision is to redefine the seafood experience, offering not just a meal but a journey that ignites your senses. Sea Flare stands out as a haven for those seeking an unparalleled gastronomic adventure.

At Sea Flare, we pride ourselves on curating the finest selection of seafood, prepared with an artistry that reflects our passion for culinary excellence. Beyond our commitment to serving exceptional dishes, we are dedicated to providing a seamless dining experience. Whether you choose to savor the flavors in our cozy dine-in area, enjoy the convenience of takeaway, or have our culinary delights delivered to your doorstep, Sea Flare is here to exceed your expectations.

Our journey began with a deep-rooted love for the ocean's bounty and a vision to share this passion with the world. Today, Sea Flare stands as a testament to our commitment to quality, innovation, and exceptional service.

Explore our multiple locations, strategically nestled along the picturesque beach sides of Sri Lanka. Each venue is designed to encapsulate the essence of Sea Flare, providing a breathtaking backdrop to your seafood experience.

Join us on this culinary voyage where passion meets plate, and your dining experience is nothing short of extraordinary.
